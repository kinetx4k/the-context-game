📖 How to Play

🥚 Hatching Eggs

Buy eggs to hatch new pets. Every pet generates Income per second. The rarer the pet, the more money it brings! Whenever you pull a pet, its probability goes down slightly, and all other pets in that egg become easier to get.

⭐ Leveling Up

Pets level up automatically over time. As their level increases, their income multiplier goes up! Watch the yellow XP bar fill up under your pets in the inventory.

⚡ Super Pet Slots

Drag and drop your best pets into the Super Pet Slots. Pets placed here gain Experience (XP) three times as fast! You can unlock more slots in the Upgrades section and even buy an Auto-Trainer!

🔮 Fusion Shrine

Drag 3 identical pets (same animal, same star level) into the 3 fusion slots. Click 'FUSE' to merge them into a new, incredibly powerful pet with a higher Star rating (up to 5 ⭐). Fused pets earn 3x more income and level up much faster!

⚙️ Upgrades & Saving

Buy upgrades to boost global Income, XP gain, unlock new slots, or automate your game. Don't forget to click the ⚙️ icon in the top left to generate and save your progress code before leaving!
