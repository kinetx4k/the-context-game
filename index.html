📖 How to Play

🥚 Hatching Eggs

Buy eggs to hatch new pets. Eggs now have a hatching cooldown! If you buy multiple, they will queue up and hatch one after another. The rarer the pet, the more money it brings per second.

⭐ Leveling Up

Pets level up automatically over time. In this Hardcore Edition, leveling requires more XP, and income starts much lower. You will need to fuse pets to survive!

⚡ Super Pet Slots

Drag and drop your best pets into the Super Pet Slots. Pets placed here gain Experience (XP) three times as fast! You can unlock more slots in the Upgrades section and even buy an Auto-Trainer!

🔮 Fusion Shrine

Drag 3 identical pets (same animal, same star level) into the 3 fusion slots. Click 'FUSE' to merge them into a new, incredibly powerful pet with a higher Star rating (up to 5 ⭐). Fused pets earn massive income multipliers!

⚙️ Upgrades & Functions

Buy upgrades to boost global Income, XP gain, unlock new slots, or automate your game. There are new upgrades to reduce Egg Hatch Times and increase Fusion Power. Look out for the manual Clicker Button!
